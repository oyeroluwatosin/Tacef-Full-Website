Wish to know more about Us

The Lord has been so gracious to the house of Tacef, as He gathers us yearly to unpack to us the things that matters in the kingdom and the issues that make our work and services acceptable unto him.(Roman 12:1-2,Isaiah 58:12)

In the Past year,the Lord has called our attention to identifying and fixing gaps; these are gaps that weaken our relationship with God and mankind and ulimately make us unfruitful in our works. The presence of these gaps are often a reflection of basic foundational flaws. This is the reason why the Lord is gathering us this year to trest our life wholesomely, so that He can check, examine and scrutinize our foundation, to see whatever went wrong and grant grace to fix them

The Lord raised TACEF to be a Repairer of the Breach and to raise the foundation of many generations (Isaiah 58:12). The foundation is critical to the success and substainability of ny structure, the strength of any building is directly related to the strength of its foundation. It takes a solid foundation to substain a great edifice, so it takes someone witha a solid foundation to stand aright in all matters of righteousness and to raise others. Therefore, if our foundation is faulty,then we cannot lay sure and solid foundation for the work of revivial tha the Lord wants to acheive in the African Church, we can only give what we have ,whatever that the Lord has acheived in us. He can acheived through us with the help of the Holy Spirit

The Lord is bringing us again unto himself to address our foundations, just like He called Jacob to return to Bethel,(Gen 35:1) we need to return to the place of our encounter with the Lord and cross-check, whether our convictions of year(s) pastare still standing firm on the solid Rock-Jesus, or whether the issues of life had affected our beliefs and shifted our once solid foundation. The scripture says "If the foundation be destroyed what can the righteous do"(Psalm 11:3). There issues/challenges of life come to everyone to test the strength of their foundations, those whose foundation is laid on the Solid Rock always overcome, while those who laid theirs on the sinking sand collapsed under challenges.(Matt 7:27).

This convention is a time to allow the Lord to x-ray to our foundation, even though our lives in the outer space appear solid but, perhaps the inner strength has been so depleted and there is a feeling 'soon to collapse'; the Great Repairer is set to fix that life from its foundation, if we sincerely call unto as the men of Jericho did(2 Kings 2:19-21)

No hiding from the Master, allow Him to expose the rot that may be deep inside and let him repair, remould and restore every foundational flaws, wheresoever it may be found, be it our prayer, bible study,evangelism,holiness,social,financial life etc. If we allow him to work on us and re-establish our foundation on the Solid Rock,only then shall we be able to raise other foundation ha will stand the test of time.

The only way to continually keep your foundation on the Solid Rock is to be the Doer of his word. Make up your mind to live according to the word of the God. There will always be rain, the flood and storms of life but if you live by the standard of the word of God you shall not be shaken..

Welcome to an encounter with the Great Repairer.
Welcome to the time of sure and Solid Foundation.
